ABOUT US

Flava Fest is a healthy eating and wellness initiative that serves as an add-on for food festivals around Ireland. It emphasises health, wellness, inclusivity, and interaction, with a specific focus on self-paced learning and gamification.

Flava Fest has a goal centred on healthy eating and wellness, encouraging interaction with food vendors at various stands throughout the festival. A nutrition passport, available in both digital and physical forms, enables attendees to collect stamps by engaging in interactive AR quizzes, teaching nutritional value beneficial to both school children and adults.
